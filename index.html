About this app

As a student of the french language I, like many others, struggle a lot when using the names of countries. Like nouns, countries have genders as well and that means you have to learn the names and their gender by heart. This app will help you in an easy and fun way.

I decided to build the app after a discussion on the r/french subreddit after I shared a worldmap which depicts every country by their gender. Some people argued that it wasn't very helpful that the map was in English and there were some mistakes also.

The app is far from ready, but you can test your skills on the list of European countries. Using this app is free, and I do not collect and/or store any user data. The data derives from a few Wikipedia pages.
